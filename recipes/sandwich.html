Sandwich

A sandwich is a versatile and convenient meal made by layering a variety of fillings between slices of bread or other bread substitutes like buns, rolls, or wraps. The fillings can include a combination of vegetables, meats, cheeses, spreads, and condiments, offering a balance of flavors and textures. Sandwiches can be served cold, like a classic vegetable or turkey sandwich, or hot, like a grilled cheese or panini. Theyâ€™re easy to customize, making them a popular choice for breakfast, lunch, snacks, or even dinner. Perfect for on-the-go meals or a cozy sit-down treat!

Ingredients

  • Bread: White, wheat, multigrain, buns, wraps.
  • Spreads: Butter, mayo, mustard, pesto, cream cheese.
  • Proteins: Chicken, ham, tuna, eggs, paneer, cheese.
  • Veggies: Lettuce, tomato, cucumber, onion, avocado.
  • Extras: Pickles, seasonings, ketchup, hot sauce.

Instructions

  1. Prepare Bread: Take 2 slices of bread or your preferred base. Toast if desired.
  2. Spread: Apply butter, mayo, or your favorite spread evenly on the slices.
  3. Add Fillings: Layer veggies, protein, and cheese as per your choice.
  4. Season: Sprinkle salt, pepper, or other seasonings.
  5. Assemble: Place the second slice of bread on top.
  6. Serve: Cut into halves or quarters if preferred and enjoy!
